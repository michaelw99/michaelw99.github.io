RUNNING BECAUSE I'M SCARED (REQUIEM)

❮ ❯

Running Because I'm Scared (Requiem) horror-themed infinite runner that I developed mostly over the course of a weekend with a friend in Unreal 5. The idea was simple: create an infinite runner that is both more challenging and stressful than others in the genre. We hoped to accomplish this mostly with one key mechanic - you must turn around in order to repel the monster chasing you to survive.

Other features in the game include:

  • Spooky retro horror aesthetic
  • Music that increases in intensity the closer you are to losing
  • Scaling difficulty over time
  • Checkpoints where you can obtain upgrades
  • Over a dozen level unique level segements

I was primarily responsible for all systems and level design. You can find the game here.
