ULTRALIFE

❮ ❯

Ultralife is a sci-fi fantasy rail shooter that follows detective Erik as he unravels the machinations of a magitech conglemerate that runs society from the shadows. Our design goal is to capture the high-octane energy of a traditional rail shooter while introducing more strategic elements with unique enemy types, weapons, and magic. This is built to be played with a Sinden light gun. Currently WIP.

I primarily work on:

  • Enemy types and AI
  • Shooting, weapons, magic
  • Wave and movement systems
